# Email Subscription Feature

Welcome to the Email Subscription Feature project repository! This project is designed to implement an email subscription feature on a website or application. It allows users to enter their email address and subscribe to receive updates, newsletters, or other information.

## Features

- **Email Subscription Form**: Users can enter their email address and subscribe to receive updates or newsletters.
- **Validation**: Validate user-entered email addresses to ensure they are correctly formatted.
- **Data Storage**: Store user email addresses securely for further communication.
- **Customizable**: Easily integrate the email subscription feature into your website or application by customizing the form and functionality.
